Tapenade (Olive Spred)

This is a really simple dish to prepare and It's always a big hit at parties. My father recomendents:

"Make this the night before so that the flavors have time to blend. Just bring it up to room tempeature before you serve it. In the winter, try serving it warm"

Ingredientes

  • 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes
  • large garlic cloves
  • 2/3 C. kalamata olives
  • 1 t. capers

Instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes and garlic in a food processor. Bkend until as smoot as possible
  2. Add capers and olives. Pulse the motor a few times until they are incorporated, but still retain some texture.
  3. Serve on thin toas reounds with goat cheese and fresh basil garnish (optional).
